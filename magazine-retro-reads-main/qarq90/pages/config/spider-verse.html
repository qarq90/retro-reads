All of the Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse news weve heard so far

By Christian Harper , Aug 8 2023

Annihilate - Spiderverse

Miles Morales in Mumbatten from Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

Spider-verse Trilogy

Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse was meant to bring Miles Morales back into our lives in March 2024, but the new movie has been indefinitely delayed.

Miles Morales VS Miguel O'hara from Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

As a result of the ongoing writers' and actors' strikes in Hollywood, Beyond the Spider-Verse has been taken off Sony's release schedule for now, likely because production wasn't completed on the movie.

It means we don't currently know when to expect the threequel, but at least Across the Spider-Verse is now available to buy on digital, so you could just endlessly rewatch that as we wait.

Peter B. Parker and Spiderman Noir from Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

Prior Intel

Before the release date delay, writers/producers Phil Lord and Chris Miller had told Rolling Stone in June 2023 that they were "far along", so hopefully it won't be too long after the strikes that the movie can be completed and, maybe, we'll still see it in 2024.

Morales family talking with Gwen Stacy from Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

Beyond the Spider-Verse release date: When can we expect Spider-Verse 3?

Delays were always a possibility, and now it's confirmed audiences will have to wait a little bit longer to see Miles Morales again on screen.

How long? We could be talking about late 2024, maybe even 2025, but nothing has been officially confirmed yet.

Fight with The Spot in Mumbatten from Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

Upcoming Characters

Theres not a lot of official Spider-Verse casting news out there at the moment but we can safely assume that ... pretty much all of the cast of Across the Spider-Verse will return, since none of their stories really wrapped up.

Different Characters from the films

Cast

Shameik Moore and Hailee Steinfeld will return as Miles Morales and Gwen Stacy, respectively, and they'll likely be joined by Oscar Isaac as Miguel O'Hara (Spider-Man 2099) , Issa Rae as Jessica Drew (Spider-Woman) and Daniel Kaluuya as Hobie Brown (Spider-Punk).

A Still of Miles wanting a suit for himself from Spiderman: Into The Spiderverse

A Still of Miguel O'hara aka Spiderman 2099 from Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

MAJOR SPOILERS - Beyond the Spider-Verse plot: What can we expect from Spider-Verse 3?

Miles is stuck in Earth-42 and at the mercy of that universe's Miles Morales who is the Prowler. In that universe, his mother is going out with his uncle Aaron Davis and his father is dead.

What we know?

That universe is one without a Spider-Man, as the spider that bit our Miles was transported to his universe (Earth-1610) by The Spot.

Luckily, help might be on the way for Miles because, as mentioned above, Gwen now has her own Spidey team to go and rescue him once they work out where he is.

Spiderverse Characters

The Spot

Spot's Plan

Miles needs to get back to his own universe, as The Spot is planning to kill his father when he becomes police captain. It's set to be a "canon event" in his timeline which Miles wants to stop, but Miguel wants to see happen to protect the Spider-Verse.

We can expect Miles, Gwen and co. versus Miguel, Jessica and the rest of the Spider Society to form the basis of Beyond the Spider-Verse with Miles trying to save his father. It's a battle that might even go to places we've never seen before.

Gwen's Fate

Like the sequel introduced a different version of Miles, the threequel is set to feature at least one other version of Gwen Stacy. "I would say that, yes. It's on the page. I think that... these things evolve as they go," Miller told the Crew Call podcast.

He also teased that we might see the death of Gwen Stacy storyline from the comics play out with one of these versions: "I'm not gonna say anything But it is sort of plot-integral, I would say."
